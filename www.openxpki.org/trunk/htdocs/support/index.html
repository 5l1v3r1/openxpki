OpenXPKI Support

For users and developers the official Mailing Lists and SourceForge tools (such as the bug tracker) are the primary resources for support. Please refer to the Resources section for details.

OpenXPKI is capable of being used in professional environments and can be highly customized to project needs. In this case you may be interested in companies offering commercial support for OpenXPKI.
